Check Password Strength

    A good password should be

  • At least 12 charcters long
  • At least 1 number
  • At least 1 uppercare letter
  • At least 1 lowercase letter
  • At least 1 special character

Other ways to nake your password more secure

Do not use personal data

Hackers can use your information to guess your password.

Avoid using names, birthdays or anything easy to guess.

Do not use common words

Common passwords such as "password" or "12345" can be easily cracked.

Ensure your password doesn't resemble common ones.

Change your password regularly

Data leaks can make your account vulnerable to breaches.

Make sure to change your password every few months.
